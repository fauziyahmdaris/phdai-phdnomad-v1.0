## 🎯 **Quick Start Guide (5 Minutes)**

### Step 1: Access the Platform
1. Visit **https://drphdai.my**
2. Click **"Start with Guided Setup"** (recommended for first-time users)
3. Or click **"Quick Start"** if you're familiar with research platforms

### Step 2: Create Your Account
1. Click **"Sign Up"** 
2. Choose account type:
   - **Normal User:** Standard access with email/password
   - **Freemium (Pilot):** Enhanced features for research study participants
3. Fill in your details:
   - Full Name
   - Email Address
   - Institution
   - Department (if applicable)
4. Click **"Create Account"**

### Step 3: Set Up Your First Research Project
1. Enter your research title
2. Select your research area from the dropdown
3. Add research objectives (you can add multiple)
4. Click **"Start Research Journey"**

## 🔧 **Core Features Overview**

### 1. **Research Navigator** 🧭
**Purpose:** Find and discover relevant academic literature

**How to Use:**
1. Go to **Research Navigator** from the sidebar
2. Enter your research keywords
3. Click **"Generate AI Keywords"** for intelligent suggestions
4. Use suggested keywords to search Google Scholar directly
5. Explore academic databases for specialized searches

**Pro Tips:**
- Use Boolean operators (AND, OR, NOT) for refined searches
- Try different keyword combinations
- Set up Google Scholar alerts for ongoing updates

### 2. **Literature Matrix** 📊
**Purpose:** Organize and analyze research papers with AI insights

**How to Use:**
1. Click **"Add Literature"** 
2. Fill in paper details:
   - Title, Authors, Year, Journal
   - **Important:** Paste the complete abstract
3. Click **"Analyze & Add to Matrix"**
4. Review AI-generated analysis:
   - Research gaps identified
   - Key findings extracted
   - Methodology summary
   - Theoretical framework
5. Use filters to organize entries
6. Export as CSV for external use

**AI Analysis Includes:**
- ✅ Research gap identification
- ✅ Key findings extraction
- ✅ Methodology analysis
- ✅ Theoretical framework identification
- ✅ Population/sample details

### 3. **Thesis Weaver** ✍️
**Purpose:** Generate references and AI-powered literature synthesis

**How to Use:**
1. Select literature entries using checkboxes
2. Choose between two modes:
   - **References:** Generate APA-style citations
   - **Synthesis:** Create AI-powered literature review
3. Click the appropriate generate button
4. Copy or download the generated content
5. **Important:** Always review and refine AI outputs

**Best Practices:**
- Start with 3-5 related papers for synthesis
- Use synthesis as a starting point, not final product
- Always verify and cite sources properly

### 4. **AI Companion** 🤖
**Purpose:** Direct access to ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Gemini with research prompts

**How to Use:**
1. Browse 10 AI-powered research tools
2. Click any tool card to open the external platform
3. Copy the provided prompt template
4. Customize with your research details
5. Replace placeholders like [TOPIC] and [FIELD]

**Available Tools:**
- Research Ideas Brainstorming
- Literature Review Assistance
- Research Methodology Suggestions
- Fact Checking Sources
- Finding Latest Research
- Identifying Top Scholars
- LR Matrix Synthesis
- Abstract Writing
- Grant Application Support

**Requirements:** You need your own accounts with ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Gemini

### 5. **Gap Scanner & Microlearning** 🔬 **(NEW FEATURE)**
**Purpose:** Revolutionary FRIN-Scanning Engine with integrated microlearning

**How to Use:**
1. Navigate to **Gap Scanner & Microlearning**
2. Choose from four tabs:
   - **FRIN Scanner:** Upload PDFs to detect research gaps
   - **Gap Map:** Visualize research clusters
   - **Decolonization Audit:** Analyze reference diversity
   - **Microlearning:** Unlock bite-sized learning modules

**FRIN Scanner:**
1. Upload research paper (PDF)
2. Click **"Run FRIN Scan"**
3. Review identified gaps with confidence scores
4. Get suggestions for addressing gaps

**Microlearning Modules:**
- Gap Hunter Fundamentals (4 mins)
- FRIN Identification Mastery (6 mins)
- Theoretical Gap Analysis (5 mins)
- Niche Spotting Tactics (7 mins)
- Decolonize Your Literature Review (8 mins)
- Advanced Gap Tactics (10 mins)

### 6. **Academic Consultants Marketplace** 👥 **(NEW FEATURE)**

**How to Use:**
1. Browse verified consultants by expertise
2. Filter by specialization, price range, or availability
3. Pay one-time $20 access fee

5. Receive personalized guidance

**Available Expertise:**

## 🔧 **Troubleshooting**

### **Common Issues & Solutions**

**Problem:** AI analysis not working  
**Solution:** Ensure complete abstract is pasted; check internet connection

**Problem:** Can't access external AI tools  
**Solution:** Create accounts on ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Gemini platforms

**Problem:** Literature matrix not saving  
**Solution:** Check browser settings; enable JavaScript; try different browser

**Problem:** Export function not working  
**Solution:** Allow downloads in browser; clear cache if needed

**Problem:** Search results not relevant  
**Solution:** Refine keywords using Boolean operators; try AI keyword generator
