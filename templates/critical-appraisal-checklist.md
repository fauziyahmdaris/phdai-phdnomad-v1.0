# Critical Appraisal Checklist (Module 1)

Use this checklist to evaluate the quality and credibility of research studies.

## Study Design & Appropriateness
- Is the study design appropriate for the research question?
- Are the aims and hypotheses clearly stated?

## Sampling & Participants
- Is the sampling strategy justified and adequate?
- Are inclusion/exclusion criteria explicit?
- Is the sample size justified (power, saturation)?

## Data Collection & Instruments
- Are instruments/tools valid and reliable?
- Are procedures described clearly and replicably?

## Bias & Confounding
- Are potential biases identified and minimized?
- Are confounders measured/controlled?

## Analysis & Rigor
- Are statistical/qualitative methods appropriate and transparent?
- Are assumptions checked? Is coding/triangulation described?

## Ethics
- Are ethics approvals mentioned?
- Is informed consent addressed? Confidentiality maintained?

## Results & Interpretation
- Are results presented clearly with effect sizes/quotes?
- Do conclusions align with data? Are limitations acknowledged?

## Generalisability & Implications
- Are contextual limits discussed?
- Are practical/theoretical implications clear?
