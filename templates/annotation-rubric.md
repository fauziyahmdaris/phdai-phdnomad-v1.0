# Annotation Rubric (Module 1)

Use this rubric to assess the depth and quality of literature annotations.

## Criteria

1. Identification of Key Claims
- Finds central thesis and main arguments
- Flags supporting evidence and counter-claims

2. Methods Critique
- Notes design, sampling, instruments
- Evaluates appropriateness and limitations

3. Evidence Evaluation
- Assesses strength and consistency across studies
- Distinguishes correlation vs causation

4. Relevance to Research Gap
- Connects findings to your gap and rationale
- Notes implications for your study

5. Synthesis Links
- Makes cross-paper connections
- Identifies agreements, disagreements, tensions

## Scoring Guide
- 0 = Not evidenced
- 1 = Emerging
- 2 = Proficient
- 3 = Advanced
